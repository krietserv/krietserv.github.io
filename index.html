kriet. just do what you like.

A space with ideal conditions for conducting tank, armed, and territorial warfare within a modded Minecraft environment. Here, every player can deploy their own strategy, build fortifications, establish logistics, and take part in large-scale battles. Thanks to advanced modifications, the vehicles and weapons are close to real-life counterparts, and operating them requires tactics and coordination.
